Eddy
Eddy is the founder of Eddy's on Coventry Salon and Eddy's Barbershop. He has over 20 years of experience as stylist and is highly skilled in all aspects of mens haircutting. He has advanced training from Vidal Sassoon and Toni & Guy. In his free time, Eddy loves to spend time on his boat overlooking the sunsets, hanging with friends while enjoying a great glass of wine and spending quality time with Kathleen, his wife of 20 year.
Melissa
Launching her career over a decade ago, Melissa, a senior stylist, is a sheer slinging force to be reckoned with. Trained by industry standards such as Paul Mitchell, Vidal Sassoon, and Aquage. Her combined knowledge and experience, paired with an artistic background, provides a bespoke end-product thats feels scuplted and custom tailored; never of the shelf.
Rachel
Rachel is a junior stylist and a graduate of Paul Mitchell The School in Cleveland. Her work is deeply rooted in her love of the arts and she has a knack for innovative cuts and enjoys working with all hair types. Rachel’s work is creative and edgy and includes a modern but feminine touch. Rachel is sought after for her hair painting and dimensional balayage techniques. Her experience in color placement provides a continued 'fresh out of the salon' feel. Rachel also has her certification in extensions. Outside the salon she enjoys painting, music, fitness, and gaming.
Vee
Vee was born in Bosnia, and grew up most of her life in Germany. She moved to the states in 1998 and has been a Clevelander ever since. After graduation, she attened Brown Aveda Institute and now has over 10+ years experience as a stylist. Continuing her education and staying current with the latest styles has always been a priority to Vee. She attended Orlando Premier multiple times, Aquage Academy, and had extensive training with Morrocan Oil Acedamy in New York City. She is very passionate about making people look and feel their best, and understanding the clients everyday lifestyle to match their look!
Brittany
Brittany is a graduate of the Brown Aveda Instistue and has been cutting and styling for ten years. Brittany now specializes in mens cutting and is your go-to person if you are looking for a good laugh. When she's not at the salon, she enjoys singing with her local band 'Top Dog', and raising her 4 year old son, Leo.
Lori
Shop momma Lori will be there to handle all of your needs. Her passion for our customers is beyond ordinary. She will make sure your appointments are booked properly and you are with the best stylist to handle your wants and needs. She will also make sure you leave our salon with not only a smile on your face, but a belly full of cookies. Give Lori a call to book an appointment with one of our many talented stylist.
