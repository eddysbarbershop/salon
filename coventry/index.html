Eddy
Eddy is the founder of Eddy's on Coventry Salon and Eddy's Barbershop. He has over 30 years of experience as stylist and is highly skilled in all aspects of mens haircutting. He has advances training from Vidal Sassoon and Toni & Guy. In his free time, Eddy loves to spend time on his boat overlooking the sunsets, hanging with friends while enjoying a great glass of wine and spending quality time with Kathleen, his wife of 20 year.
Renee
The beauty industry is nothing new to Renee. While growing up in the industry, Renee discovered her passion for making people look and feel beautiful. She attended vocational school and graduated in 2009. Renee advanced her education with extensive training from Aveda, Aquage, Keratin Complex and Deva Curl. She now specializes in womens cuts and colors and enjoys working with all hair textures. Her versatility has lead to loyal client base.
Melissa
Launching her career over a decade ago, Melissa, a senior stylist, is a sheer slinging force to be reckoned with. Trained by industry standards such as Paul Mitchell, Vidal Sassoon, and Aquage. Her combined knowledge and experience, paired with an artistic background,provides a bespoke end-product thats feels scuplted and custom tailored; never of the shelf.
Roberta
After ten years behind the chair in her hometown of Columbus, Roberta took her talents, her handsome husband, her wickedly cute son,and in the spring of 2014, moved to Cleveland, Ohio. Her passions include giving clients and subsequently fawning over perfectly manicured eyebrows, short and textured razor cuts, for both men and women, and drinking ice cold beers while watching Cubs games. In her off time, Roberta moonlights as a professional ball-buster.
Vee
Vee was born in Bosnia, and grew up most of her life in Germany. She moved to the states in 1998 and has been a Clevelander ever since. After graduation, she attened Brown Aveda Institute and now has over 10+ years experience as a stylist. Continuing her education and staying current with the latest styles has always been a priority to Vee. She attended Orlando Premier multiple times, Aquage Academy, and had extensive training with Morrocan Oil Acedamy in New York City. She is very passionate about making people look and feel their best, and understanding the clients everyday lifestyle to match their look!
Brittany
Brittany is a graduate of the Brown Aveda Instistue and has been cutting and styling for ten years. Brittany now specializes in mens cutting and is your go-to person if you are looking for a good laugh. When she's not at the salon, she enjoys singing with in her local band 'Top Dog', spending time with her big Italian family and raising her son 4 year old son, Leo.
