EXCELLENT SERVICE

Eddy's offers exceptional hair services in a relaxed, chic setting. With our individualized approach to styling and color for both men and women, we excel in personalized attention and an inspired look.

EXPERIENCE

Every Eddy's stylist specializes in both haircuts and color and tailors their technique according to your hair type and facial structure—enhancing your complete image.

WE LOVE OUR JOBS

And we especially love making you look and feel your best. While other salons may provide you with quality service, many of the little details get lost in the shuffle.
