Renee
Driven and passionate, Renee Dreshaj is one of the best young talents in the Cleveland hair scene. Following her mother around the salon from the age of ten, you could say she grew up in the industry and even received her formal cosmetology training and licensing while still in high school. With a focused dedication and pure love for her craft Renee has progressed from an assistant to a master stylist with her own salon. Renee has extensive experience with many product and color lines including Aveda, Aquage, DevaCurl, Keratin Complex, Schwarzkopf, Redken, and her favorite Kevin Murphy. She is very result focused and loves to bring a creative element for each client often combining classic techniques with new styles. Renee’s versatility in working with all hairstyles and textures, along with her devotion to the best results, have produced an extremely loyal client base. Renee’s care for others extends beyond clients, friends, and family. She cares deeply for the environment and the impact our daily practices have on those around us. She uses only cruelty-free products in her salon and is in the process of becoming a green circle salon.
Jaclyn
After giving herself a haircut at the age of 5, Jaclyn knew the beauty industry was going to be apart of her life. Graduating from the brown aveda institute in 2012, Jaclyn continued to her education with advanced from training from Schwarzkopf, Deva Curl and Vidal Sassoon. She now specializes in personalized hair color and mens cutting. Jaclyn is fueled by the energy of her clients to make them look and feel their best. In her free time Jaclyn enjoys being active outside and spending time with her family.
Meridyth
With over 14 years of experience in the industry, Meridyth has set a strong path for herself creating client specific colors and cuts. She specialized in balayage highlights, razor cuts, and custom colors. Her passion for the industry is shown as she strives to always meet her clients specific style and needs. She puts much emphasis on attending classes to stay current in the industry and that is shown through her beautiful work!
Abby
Abby has been a stylist in the industry for 11 years. Her passion for all things hair radiates through her creativity and attention to detail. She loves to connect with her clients in a way to truly understand their vision keeping in mind what works best for their specific needs. Abby perfects her craft through advanced training which allows her to be versatile with all hair types and textures. She specializes in custom color, balayage and cuts for both men and women.
